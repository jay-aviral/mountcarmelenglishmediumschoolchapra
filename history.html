Our Glorious History

1995 - The Humble Beginnings

Mount Carmel English Medium School was established in 1995 with just 47 students and 3 teachers in a small rented building. Founded by the Carmelite Sisters of Charity, the school began with a vision to provide quality English-medium education to the children of Chapra. The first principal, Sister Mary Joseph, laid the foundation of our value-based education system that continues today.

2005 - A Decade of Growth

By our 10th anniversary, we had grown to 350 students and moved to our permanent campus on Station Road. This period saw the construction of our first science lab and library. The school gained recognition for academic excellence when our students secured top positions in the district board exams. Our annual cultural festival "Carmel Utsav" was established during this time, becoming a much-anticipated event in Chapra.

2020 - The Modern Institution

Today, Mount Carmel stands as a premier educational institution with over 1,200 students and 68 faculty members. Our campus now includes smart classrooms, computer labs, and sports facilities. The school has won the "Best School in Nadia District" award three times consecutively. We take pride in our alumni who have excelled in fields from medicine to engineering across India and abroad, while maintaining the core values instilled during their time at Mount Carmel.

Our Continuing Legacy

Through 25+ years of educational service, Mount Carmel has remained committed to our founding principles of "Knowledge with Character". Each year, we welcome new generations of students while maintaining the traditions that have made us beloved in the Chapra community. As we look to the future, we continue to innovate in education while preserving the values that have guided us since 1995.
