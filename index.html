Welcome to Mount Carmel English Medium School

Founded in 1995, Mount Carmel English Medium School has been providing quality education to the children of Chapra for over two decades. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated faculty create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We believe in nurturing young minds to become responsible global citizens.
