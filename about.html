Mount Carmel English Medium School in Chapra has been a beacon of quality education since its establishment in 1995. Our institution is committed to providing holistic development to students through a balanced curriculum that combines academic excellence with moral values and extracurricular activities. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and dedicated faculty, we create an environment where every child can thrive.

At Mount Carmel, we believe education should go beyond textbooks. Our innovative teaching methodologies incorporate hands-on learning experiences, digital classrooms, and project-based assignments. The school regularly organizes science exhibitions, literary competitions, and cultural events to foster creativity and critical thinking among students.

The school boasts exceptional facilities including well-equipped laboratories, a spacious library with over 10,000 books, and modern sports infrastructure. Our computer lab with high-speed internet access ensures students are technologically proficient. The campus features separate play areas for primary and senior students, along with indoor game facilities.

Our faculty comprises highly qualified and experienced teachers who undergo regular training to stay updated with the latest educational trends. The teacher-student ratio of 1:25 ensures personalized attention for each child. Special remedial classes are conducted for students who need extra support in particular subjects.

Mount Carmel takes pride in its vibrant student community that actively participates in social initiatives like tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and charity events. The school has won numerous awards at district and state level competitions, reflecting our commitment to all-round excellence. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to nurturing responsible global citizens.
